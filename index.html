WELCOME TO Agüita HOUSE


Imagine surfing with the horizon behind you and a huge wave chasing you, a wave beyond your capabilities. But surprisingly, you catch it. Local Canarians express their excitement with the simple word: "Aguita!".


Those are the sensations that are waiting for you in this place: surprise, excitement and fun. Aguita House can offer you the purest "home" vibe, thanks to its loving staff and exclusive location, right in front of one of the most beautiful beaches in Europe. Our rooftop has a breathtaking view that encompasses all the shades of blue that the Atlantic Ocean can offer. Imagine how many twinkles of light you could try to count when the sun sets behind Teide!

SURF LESSONS

“Oceanside is a company from Las Palmas de Gran Canaria (Canary Islands, Spain) in which everything revolves around this wonderful activity called surfing. We began as a surf shop on Las Canteras Beach more than 10 years ago, and since then we haven’t stopped growing. Currently we run the Quiksilver & Roxy Gran Canaria Surf School, a surf travel agency and a stand up paddle Center.
‘surfaris’ to the island’s best waves, summer camps for children up to the age of fifteen, surfing, bodyboarding and paddleboarding lessons, customised training for advanced surfers and equipment hire of all kind. All in all, we have everything you need to enjoy your stay on the island of Gran Canaria to the full.”

Find out more on https://oceansidegrancanaria.com/
